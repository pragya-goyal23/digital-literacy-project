Stay Safe Online - Prevention Checklist

- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Always verify the sender before responding to emails or messages
- Never share OTP, passwords, or bank details with anyone
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible
- Carefully check UPI payment requests before approving them
- Do not scan random QR codes, especially for receiving money
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or financial transactions
